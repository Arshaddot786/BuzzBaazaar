Where we operate

Karachi. Lahore. Islamabad.
All covered.

BuzzBaazaar is headquartered in Karachi and operates campaigns across Pakistan's three major cities. Each city has its own high-footfall locations, demographics, and campaign opportunities — and we know all of them.

Location tiers

We know where your audience actually is

TIER A — MAX FOOTFALL

Public roads & chowks

High-traffic intersections, major roads, and bus stops. Widest reach. Best for mass awareness, heatwave relief, and cap distribution targeting all demographics.

TIER B — TARGETED

University gates & campuses

Concentrated youth demographic. Highest engagement rate for quizzes and content shoots. Best for telecom, FMCG, fintech, and fashion brands targeting 18–28.

TIER C — PREMIUM

Malls & commercial areas

Higher income bracket, longer dwell time, purchase-ready mindset. Best for in-store sampling, product demos, and premium brand perception campaigns.

Multi-city campaigns

One brief. Three cities. Maximum noise.

For brands wanting national coverage, BuzzBaazaar can coordinate simultaneous or staggered campaigns across all three cities. One point of contact. One campaign brief. Three-city execution.

Simultaneous deployment

All three cities activated on the same day. Maximum national PR impact. Ideal for product launches, brand announcements, or CSR campaigns that need to show nationwide reach.

Staggered city rollout

Start in Karachi, build momentum, then roll to Lahore and Islamabad over 3 weeks. Content from each city feeds into the brand's social channels and builds anticipation.

City-specific campaigns

Run different campaign types per city based on local demographics. Karachi gets the heatwave campaign. Lahore gets the street content shoot. Islamabad gets the brand perception survey.
