Heatwave season

The heat is on. Own the moment.

Every May–July, millions of Karachi residents face 44°C+ with no brand-sponsored relief. That gap is yours to own. BuzzBaazaar deploys heatwave relief campaigns that create genuine public goodwill, earned media, and CSR documentation — all under your brand identity.

Water Station

Branded cooler, canopy, and cups at high-footfall locations. 500+ people served daily. Every cup carries your logo home.

Misting Fan Zone + Quiz

Portable misting fans create a branded cool zone. Live quiz with prizes drives crowd engagement and generates shareable content.

ORS & Electrolyte Giveaway

Branded hydration sachets at bus stops and chowks. Brand supplies product; we film, distribute, and capture leads.

Shade Tent Street Station

Large branded canopy with seating and heatstroke awareness cards at busy chowks. Maximum CSR visibility and news coverage.

Copen Ice Cream Run

Branded convertible drives through neighbourhoods distributing cold products from the car. Filmed throughout. A sampling reel delivered.
