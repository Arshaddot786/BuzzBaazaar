The process

Simple. Fast. No wasted time.

Most BTL agencies take 3–6 weeks to deploy. We do it in 5 days. Here is exactly how every BuzzBaazaar campaign goes from your first call to live on the street.

01

Free strategy call (Day 0)

20 minutes on WhatsApp or a call. We listen to your brand goals, your target audience, and your locations. We recommend the exact campaign type and duration that will deliver results — no hard sell, no generic proposal. You leave with a clear plan.

02

Brief approved (Day 1)

You confirm the campaign type, package duration, and locations. Send your brand logo, colour codes, and any product guidelines. We get to work immediately.

03

Production (Day 2)

Branded cups, standees, canopy panels, and cap print files go to our production partners. Copen wrap design finalised. Brand ambassadors briefed. Quiz questions written and loaded into the platform. QR code lead form built.

04

Car wrapped + advance payment (Day 3)

Copen wrapped in your campaign identity. Rented cars collected and wrapped. Advance payment received confirms the booking. Location permissions cleared. Videographer and crew scheduled.

05

Activation goes live (Day 5+)

Full activation runs at your chosen locations. Live WhatsApp updates sent throughout the day. Roof opens. Quiz runs. Gifts distributed. Every moment filmed by our crew.

✓

Report delivered (within 48 hours)

Edited social media clips, participant count, location photos, QR lead list, and CSR documentation — all delivered within 48 hours of the final activation day. Ready for your social channels, your boss, and your CSR report.

Payment terms

Transparent. No surprises.

ADVANCE

50% to confirm booking

50% advance is required to begin production and confirm your dates. This covers wrap production, printing, and crew scheduling. Non-refundable after production begins.

BALANCE

50% on activation day 1

The remaining 50% is due on the first day of activation before the activation begins. We then run the full campaign as agreed.

WRAP SETUP

Separate one-time fee

Car wrap production is charged as a one-time campaign setup fee, separate from the day rate. This covers vinyl printing, cutting, and fitting for each branded vehicle.

What you get in every campaign

Live WhatsApp updates

Real-time photos and short clips sent to your team throughout the activation day. No waiting for the report to know what's happening.

Edited social clips

5–8 short-form clips per day, formatted for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Facebook. Brand logo and music included. Delivered in 48 hours.

QR lead list

Name, phone number, and city of every participant who scanned your campaign QR code. Clean Excel sheet delivered with the report.

Participation report

Total participant count, peak hours, location photos, and crowd size estimates. Formatted for internal brand reporting or stakeholder presentations.

CSR documentation

For heatwave and community campaigns — a formal CSR summary with photos, participant count, and narrative for your annual report.

Campaign wrap-up call

A 15-minute debrief call after the campaign ends. What worked. What we'd optimise. Recommendations for the next campaign.
