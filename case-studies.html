What brands get from us

Five things no billboard delivers

01

Public goodwill that money cannot buy

Every cup of cold water, every branded cap, every shade tent creates an emotional memory in the public mind. People remember the brand that helped them in 44°C heat — for the entire year.

02

Earned media and news coverage

A branded rooftop convertible handing out cold products in 44°C heat is a feel-good summer news story. Local channels and digital media run these stories every summer. One media pickup = coverage worth 10x the campaign cost.

03

Real social media content — not studio fiction

People film the Copen roof opening. They photograph themselves in your branded cap. They share the quiz score on their stories. Every BuzzBaazaar activation generates organic UGC that paid content cannot replicate.

04

A clean leads database from every activation

Every participant who claims a quiz prize or gift scans a QR code that captures their name and phone number. A 1,000-cap campaign becomes a 1,000-contact CRM list your brand owns.

05

CSR documentation for your annual report

Participant count, location photos, video evidence, and a written narrative — ready to drop into your annual CSR report or stakeholder presentation. No extra work on your side.
